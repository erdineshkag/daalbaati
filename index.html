🏠 Our Story

A Taste of Tradition
from Druvita's Kitchen

Welcome to Druvita's Daal Baati — where every baati is hand-rolled, wood-fire baked, and soaked in pure desi ghee. Located in the heart of Kukshi, Dhar (Madhya Pradesh), our restaurant brings the authentic flavors of Rajasthani cuisine right to your doorstep.

Owner Druvita believes that great food is made with love, patience, and the finest ingredients. Every plate tells a story of tradition passed down through generations.
